Bubble Senior High School: Where Potential Takes Flight

Bubble Senior High School empowers students in grades 9-12 to excel academically and personally. Located in Short Guy Town, we offer a rigorous curriculum taught by dedicated faculty, preparing students for future success in diverse fields.
Beyond academics, students explore passions through diverse clubs, competitive athletics, and vibrant arts programs. Our supportive, innovative community fosters respect, resilience, and global citizenship in modern facilities.
Our Mission: To challenge minds, nurture well-being, and equip students with the skills and confidence to thrive in college, career, and life.
Join us at Bubble Senior High – dream big, work hard, and make your mark!
